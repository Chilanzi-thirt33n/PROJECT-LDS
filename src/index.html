About us

Lusaka Dental Surgery began its Services in March, 2009,Founded by a dedicated team of dentists.We are dedicated to the prevention of dental disease. Our patients have the right to make their own decisions and we believe that a properly educated and motivated patient will achieve their own personal treatment goals.

All our staff are professionals in their fields and the clinical staff are very experienced (you’ll see the same faces year after year) and have completed studies required to gain their Registration with the Health Professions Council of Zamia.

Further studies keep staff highly skilled and motivated and allow us to care for our patients better.
